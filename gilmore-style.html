The Academic Muse

Rory's Core

Embodying the "cozy intellectual" vibe, Rory's style is perfect for crisp autumn mornings. Her aesthetic is built on classic staples that never go out of style.

Get the Rory Look:

  • The Knit: Look for "Aran cable knit" or "oversized cream chunky sweaters".
  • The Skirt: Search for "plaid A-line mini skirts" or "wool tartan skirts".
  • Footwear: Classic Mary Janes or brown leather loafers.
  • The Bag: A vintage-style leather backpack or a large canvas tote.

The Eclectic Queen

Lorelai's Vibe

Bold, witty, and masterfully eclectic. Lorelai combines sharp tailoring with early 2000s playfulness.

Get the Lorelai Look:

  • Coats: Search for "longline tailored wool coats" in vibrant colors like pink or blue.
  • Leather: A fitted brown leather blazer or jacket is a must.
  • Boots: "Knee-high pointed toe boots" with a comfortable block heel.
  • The Scarf: Look for "skinny knit scarves" in contrasting colors.
