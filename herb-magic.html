The Magic in Your Cupboard 🌿

You don't need a cauldron to be a witch; your kitchen is already full of ancient magic. When we cook with intention, we transform a simple meal into a ritual of protection, love, or prosperity.

Herb magic is a simple yet powerful practice. The herbs and spices we use every day have been valued for centuries not just for their flavour, but also for their mystical properties.

Cinnamon 🪵

Success, healing, and protection. Use it to speed up manifestations.

Rosemary 🌿

Cleansing, memory, and protection. Hang it over your door for peace.

Lavender 💜

Peace, sleep, and purification. Perfect for calming a chaotic home.

Cloves ☁️

Stops gossip and drives away negativity. A shield for your hearth.

Our ancestors believed that the plants had spirits and using them with respect and gratitude would unleash their true power. Treat your ingredients with care and they will reward you.

"Bless the pot, bless the flame, magic rises in my name."
