Rosewood Beauty

Secrets, Lies, and the Perfect Eyeliner

"Never trust a pretty girl with an ugly secret." — In Rosewood, makeup isn't just about beauty; it's armor. Here is the definitive guide to the Liars' signature looks.

Alison DiLaurentis

The Queen Bee. Alison's look is classic, intimidating, and perfectly polished. She relies on timeless femininity to mask her complicated nature.

  • Lips: The signature pink or classic red gloss.
  • Eyes: Soft champagne shimmer with perfectly curled lashes.
  • Face: Peachy blush for that "innocent" porcelain glow.

Aria Montgomery

The Indie Soul. Aria's makeup is as eclectic as her earrings. She isn't afraid to go dark, bold, and experimental.

  • Brows: Strong, bold, and perfectly defined.
  • Eyes: Heavy mascara and dark, smudgy kohl liner.
  • Lips: Deep berry, plum, or matte mauve tones.

Hanna Marin

The It-Girl. Hanna's look is high-glam and trendy. She masters the art of the smokey eye and the sun-kissed California glow.

  • Eyes: Bronzed smokey eye with a winged liner.
  • Face: Sharp contour and a high-shine highlighter.
  • Lips: Creamy nude or soft petal pink.

Spencer Hastings

The Academic. Spencer's look is "Old Money" and understated. It’s about looking effortless and naturally superior.

  • Face: "No-makeup" makeup. Fresh, breathable skin.
  • Eyes: Matte brown shadows to define the crease.
  • Lips: Just a touch of tinted lip balm or sheer rose.

Emily Fields

The Natural. Emily's beauty is effortless and athletic. She focuses on a radiant, dewy finish that highlights her features.

  • Face: Dewy finish with a sun-kissed bronzer.
  • Eyes: Earthy tones and waterproof mascara.
  • Glow: Emphasis on the cheekbones and bridge of the nose.

Mona Vanderwaal

The Chameleon. Mona's makeup is precise, calculated, and always sharp. She knows how to use beauty as a weapon.

  • Eyes: Sharp-as-a-knife cat eyeliner.
  • Face: Perfectly matte skin with zero imperfections.
  • Lips: Bold fuchsia or a very calculated nude.
