Cinnamon Rolls: Golden Glow & Intentions

There is nothing like the scent of cinnamon filling the house on a crisp October morning. It’s more than just baking; it’s a ritual of warmth and comfort.

🍞 The Recipe

Dough: 500g flour, 7g yeast, 250ml warm milk, 80g melted butter, 50g sugar, 1 egg.

Filling: 100g soft butter, 150g brown sugar, 3 tbsp cinnamon.

Frosting: 150g powdered sugar, 2 tbsp milk.

🪄 The Ritual (Method)

  • The Awakening: Mix warm milk, yeast, and a spoonful of sugar. Let it rest until it bubbles—that's the life waking up.
  • Gathering: Combine the yeast mix with flour, melted butter, sugar, and the egg. Knead until the dough feels like a soft pillow. Let it rise in a warm corner for 1 hour.
  • The Spiral: Roll the dough into a large rectangle. Spread the soft butter and cover it with the cinnamon-sugar mix. Roll it tightly into a log.
  • Transformation: Cut into 12 rolls and place them in a tray. Let them rise for 30 more minutes. Bake at 180°C for 20-25 minutes until golden.
  • The Final Touch: While warm, drizzle with the frosting. Watch it melt into the spirals.

✨ Spiritual Meaning

In witchcraft and kitchen alchemy, cinnamon is a powerful ally:

  • Protection & Success: Cinnamon raises the vibration of your home.
  • Sweetness of Life: Brown sugar represents harmony and the comfort of the hearth.
  • The Spiral: As you roll the dough, visualize sealing in positive intentions.
"As the rolls rise, so does the energy of peace in my home."
