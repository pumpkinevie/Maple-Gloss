Cinnamon Rolls: Golden Glow & Intentions

There is nothing like the scent of cinnamon filling the house on a crisp October morning. It’s more than just baking; it’s a ritual of warmth and comfort.

🍞 The Recipe

Dough: 500g flour, 7g yeast, 250ml warm milk, 80g melted butter, 50g sugar, 1 egg.

Filling: 100g soft butter, 150g brown sugar, 3 tbsp cinnamon.

Frosting: 150g powdered sugar, 2 tbsp milk.

🪄 The Ritual (Method)

  • The Awakening: Mix warm milk, yeast, and sugar. Let it bubble.
  • Gathering: Knead everything into a soft dough. Let it rise for 1 hour until doubled.
  • The Spiral: Roll out, spread butter and cinnamon-sugar, then roll tightly.
  • Transformation: Cut into rolls, rise for 30 mins, then bake at 180°C for 25 mins.
  • The Final Touch: Drizzle with frosting while they are still warm and glowing.

✨ Spiritual Meaning

In witchcraft and kitchen alchemy, cinnamon is a powerful ally for protection and prosperity. As you roll the dough, visualize sealing in positive intentions for the season ahead.

"As the rolls rise, so does the energy of peace in my home."
