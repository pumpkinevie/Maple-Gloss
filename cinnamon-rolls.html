Cinnamon Rolls: Golden Glow & Intentions

There is nothing like the scent of cinnamon filling the house on a crisp October morning. It’s more than just baking; it’s a ritual of warmth and comfort.

🥧 The Recipe

  • Dough: 500g flour, 7g yeast, 250ml warm milk, 80g melted butter, 50g sugar, 1 egg.
  • Filling: 100g soft butter, 150g brown sugar, 3 tbsp cinnamon.
  • Frosting: 150g powdered sugar, 2 tbsp milk.

✨ Spiritual Meaning

In witchcraft and kitchen alchemy, cinnamon is a powerful ally:

  • Protection & Success: Cinnamon raises the vibration of your home and attracts prosperity.
  • Sweetness of Life: Brown sugar represents harmony and the comfort of the hearth.
  • The Spiral: As you roll the dough, visualize yourself sealing in positive intentions and protection for the season ahead.
"As the rolls rise, so does the energy of peace in my home."
