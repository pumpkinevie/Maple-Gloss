Spring Full Moon Water ✨💧

Moon water is a simple yet powerful way to store lunar energy. In spring, this water is charged with the vibrations of growth, renewal, and new beginnings. It’s like catching starlight in a jar.

The Ritual:

  • Gather: Find a glass jar or bottle (blue or clear glass is best). Fill it with fresh water.
  • Infuse: Add crystals like Amethyst or Moonstone *around* the jar to enhance the energy.
  • Expose: Place it outside or on a windowsill under the light of the Full Moon.
  • Manifest: Whisper your spring intentions into the water before leaving it overnight.

How to use it:

Use your Moon Water to water your spring plants, add a few drops to your bath for spiritual cleansing, or use it to clean your altar space. It’s a touch of magic in your daily routine.
