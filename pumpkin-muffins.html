    Fluffy Pumpkin Spice Muffins 🎃🧁

    There is something magical about the smell of pumpkin spice filling the house on a rainy Tuesday. These muffins are soft, tall, and taste like a warm hug. It's the ultimate comfort food for a Y2K autumn vibe.

    The Essentials:

    • 1 cup Pumpkin purée
    • 2 cups Flour & 2 Eggs
    • 1/2 cup Brown sugar
    • 1 tbsp Pumpkin Spice mix
    • A handful of chocolate chips

    The Magic Process:

    Whisk the wet ingredients first, then gently fold in the dry ones. Don't overmix! We want them light and airy. Bake at 190°C for about 20 minutes until your kitchen feels like an enchanted autumn forest.

    "Everything is better with a bit of pumpkin spice and nostalgia."
